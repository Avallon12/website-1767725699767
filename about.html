We believe in conscious beauty.

Aura is more than just skincare; it's a philosophy. We're dedicated to creating products that are kind to your skin, kind to animals, and kind to the planet.

Our Mission

To empower individuals through mindful self-care rituals with clean, effective, and ethically-produced skincare.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in sustainable beauty, inspiring a community that values transparency, wellness, and environmental stewardship.

Our Values

Purity, Integrity, Kindness, and Sustainability are the pillars that support every decision we make at Aura.

Our Journey So Far

From a humble beginning to a thriving community.

2020 - The Spark

Founder, Maya Chen, begins experimenting with botanical blends in her home kitchen, searching for a solution to her own sensitive skin.

2021 - Aura is Born

After a year of formulation, Aura launches online with three core products: the Radiance Serum, Cleansing Balm, and Hydrating Mist.

2023 - Certified Cruelty-Free

Aura proudly receives Leaping Bunny certification, solidifying our commitment to animal welfare.

2024 - 50,000 Strong

Our community grows to over 50,000 happy customers worldwide, and we expand our line with five new, innovative products.
